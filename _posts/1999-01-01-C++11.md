* variadic templates
* rvalue references

* static assertions
* auto
* lambda expressions
* decltype
* template aliases
* nullptr

* attributes

* user-defined literals
* explicit virtual overrides
* final specifier

* deleted functions

* converting constructors
* explicit conversion functions
* inline namespaces
* non static data member initializers
* right angle brackets
